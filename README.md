Career Guidance;

A web-based career guidance system designed to help learners explore potential career paths based on their interests, strengths, and subject choices.

About the Project;

This platform helps students make informed career decisions by answering simple questions and suggesting suitable career paths. It is built to make career exploration easier, more interactive, and accessible for learners.

Features;

Interactive career quiz to assess interests and strengths.
Personalized career recommendations.
Simple and user-friendly interface.
Educational career descriptions.
Responsive design for mobile and desktop devices.
