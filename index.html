About Wanafunzi

Wanafunzi is an educational career guidance website that helps learners discover careers that match their interests, talents, hobbies, personality and academic performance.

Who We Are

Choosing a career is one of the biggest decisions a learner makes. Unfortunately, many students choose careers without understanding their abilities, interests or future opportunities.

Wanafunzi provides career guidance through an interactive assessment that analyses learners' interests, favourite subjects, talents and academic performance to recommend suitable careers.

The website also educates learners about different professions, helping them understand the qualifications, skills and educational pathways required for each career.

Our Mission & Vision

Helping every learner make informed career choices.

Mission

To provide every learner with reliable, easy-to-understand career guidance using technology and interactive assessments.

Vision

To become the leading digital career guidance platform that empowers learners to discover their future with confidence.

Our Goal

To reduce confusion when choosing careers and help learners align their interests, strengths and talents with suitable professions.

CBC Senior School Guide

Choose a pathway that matches your strengths

Under Kenya's Competency Based Curriculum, Grade 9 learners transition to Senior School by choosing a pathway and track. Use this guide to connect subjects, strengths and possible careers before exploring the career cards below.

STEM Pathway

Tracks: Pure Sciences, Applied Sciences, Technical Studies

Best for learners who enjoy

Science, technology, Mathematics, design, investigation, building and practical problem solving.

Supporting subjects

  • Advanced Mathematics
  • Biology, Chemistry, Physics or General Science
  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture, Aviation, Building Construction, Electricity or other technical subjects where offered

Possible careers

Doctor, nurse, pharmacist, software engineer, data analyst, pilot, architect, civil engineer, mechanic, electrician, agricultural scientist, laboratory technologist.

Social Sciences Pathway

Tracks: Languages & Literature, Humanities & Business Studies

Best for learners who enjoy

Communication, people, leadership, society, business, history, faith studies, writing and decision making.

Supporting subjects

  • English, Kiswahili and Literature in English
  • History & Citizenship, Geography, CRE/IRE/HRE
  • Business Studies
  • Foreign languages such as French, German, Arabic or Mandarin where offered

Possible careers

Teacher, lawyer, journalist, accountant, banker, entrepreneur, marketer, procurement officer, human resource officer, diplomat, social worker, public administrator.

Arts & Sports Science Pathway

Tracks: Arts, Sports

Best for learners who enjoy

Creativity, performance, design, movement, teamwork, storytelling, media, music and talent development.

Supporting subjects

  • Fine Arts, Theatre & Film, Music & Dance
  • Sports & Recreation
  • Media Technology
  • Biology, General Science, Computer Studies or Business Studies depending on the track combination

Possible careers

Graphic designer, musician, actor, film producer, animator, photographer, fashion designer, sports coach, physiotherapist pathway, media producer, interior designer.

Based on Kenya Ministry of Education Grade 10 pathway selection information and KICD Basic Education Curriculum Framework.
